CAM / Precision Milling & Machining


From Concept to Finished Part


Iridium Manufacturing offers complete CNC machining services using the latest state-of-the-art CNC machines, coupled with advanced CAM software. Our CAM tools allow for multiple tool path generation, rapid traverse on machining, and crash avoidance and protection—ensuring parts are machined correctly the first time. Combined with advanced milling capabilities, we produce complex parts efficiently and accurately. We support complex geometries and tight tolerances with ongoing quality monitoring for high-precision parts.



Our CAM / Precision Milling & Machining Process

  1. Design Review: Analyze part data, geometry, and tolerances using SolidWorks & Visi modeling
  2. CAM Programming: Generate tool paths with crash avoidance and rapid traverse optimization
  3. Precision Machining: Multi-axis machining with complete accuracy and precision
  4. Quality Verification: Monitor critical dimensions and confirm tight tolerances
