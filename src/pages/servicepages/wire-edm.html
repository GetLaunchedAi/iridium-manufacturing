Wire EDM Services


Ultra-Precise Electrical Discharge Machining


Wire EDM (Electrical Discharge Machine) is a manufacturing process that erodes a shape in metal by running a current between two electrodes. Wire EDMs are high precision machines that can hold tolerances to 0.0002" or better, making them ideal for parts requiring extreme accuracy.



Our Wire EDM Process

  2. Setup: Configure wire EDM machine with appropriate parameters
  3. Machining: Precision erosion process with continuous monitoring

Wire EDM FAQs

Answers to common questions about Wire EDM, precision capabilities, tolerances, and what to expect when working with Iridium Manufacturing.

  • Wire EDM (Electrical Discharge Machine) is a manufacturing process that erodes a shape in metal by running a current between two electrodes. It's ideal for parts requiring extreme precision and tight tolerances.

  • Our Wire EDM machines can hold tolerances to 0.0002" or better, making them suitable for the most demanding precision applications in aerospace, defense, and other industries.

  • Wire EDM works well with conductive materials including hardened steels, titanium, aluminum, and other difficult-to-machine alloys. It's particularly effective for materials that would be difficult to machine using traditional methods.

  • Wire EDM is ideal for parts with complex geometries, thin walls, sharp corners, and tight tolerances. It's commonly used for tooling, dies, and precision components across multiple industries.
